THE CLIMATE CRISIS

The Global Threat

Climate change is drastically altering global weather patterns. Unpredictable rainfall, severe droughts, and rising temperatures mean countries can no longer rely on traditional farming seasons, leading directly to mass crop failure and starvation.

Our Tech Adaptation

WeClimate is an environmental resilience app designed to adapt to erratic climates. It calculates:

  • Shifting precipitation patterns in vulnerable areas.
  • Extreme UV and sunlight exposure levels.
  • Viability and exact growth timelines for crops under new climate conditions.

HOW IT WORKS

ENVIRONMENTAL GOALS

Climate Disaster Warning

Acts as an early warning system for sudden environmental hazards like flash floods or extreme heatwaves.

Adaptive Planting

Guides farmers on the narrow windows of opportunity to plant crops in a destabilized climate.

Sustainable Harvesting

Notifies communities exactly when to harvest before erratic weather can destroy the yield.

DATA DRIVEN SURVIVAL

Tracking Environmental Variables:

  • Shifting Rainfall Volumes
  • Temperature Anomalies
  • Solar Radiation Fluctuations

Empowering the User

The app aggregates complex climate models and displays a simple, actionable set of instructions to ensure food security.

CONCLUSION

"Our conclusion is that WeClimate empowers vulnerable communities to navigate and survive extreme environmental unpredictability. Technology and data are our strongest defenses in adapting to the realities of Climate Change."
